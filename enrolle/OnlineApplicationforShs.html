Online application for Senior High School

Qualified applicant for Incoming Senior High School: You are eligible if you meet any of the following criteria:

You are currently Grade 10 Junior High School Student
You have graduated from non-formal education accreditation and equivalency (including the Alternative Learning System Accreditation and Equivalency program, view requirements here)
If you have finished Junior High School via the International Baccalaureate, Cambridge International - International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE), or an equivalent program.
